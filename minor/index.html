From the iconic Red Fort and Jama Masjid to the tranquil gardens of Lodi and the modernity of Connaught Place, every corner offered a glimpse into Delhi's rich heritage and contemporary life. Sampling street food delicacies and navigating the city's bustling streets added layers to the experience, leaving lasting impressions of Delhi's charm and energy.

The tranquil backwaters offered serene boat rides, while the Kathakali performances showcased the region's traditional arts. Kochi's unique ambiance left a lasting impression, offering a memorable travel experience filled with diversity and allure.
